Head of Base Decision

🔒

Management Section

This section is reserved for the Head of Base only.
It will be completed after the field assessment is submitted and reviewed.

The generated report includes a dedicated signature block and decision fields for management to fill in.

What happens next:Generate the report → email to Head of Base → HoB reviews, signs, and records the formal decision (Approved / Conditional / Rejected / Pending).
